## Lessons Learned

### Technical Insights

#### 1. Perfection Prevents Shipping
**Lesson**: The pursuit of perfect systems often prevents any system from shipping. "Just good enough" enables real-world deployment and value delivery.

**Implication**: Focus on production-ready solutions that solve actual problems, not theoretical ideals.

#### 2. Over-Engineering Is the Enemy
**Lesson**: Systems designed for perfection become over-engineered and difficult to maintain. "Just good enough" maintains appropriate complexity levels.

**Implication**: Design for the 75% that matters most - core functionality, reliability, usability.

#### 3. Framework Shapes AI Behavior
**Lesson**: The operating environment fundamentally shapes AI responses and reasoning. The framework isn't just used by AI - it shapes how AI operates.

**Implication**: Framework design has profound implications for AI behavior and capabilities.

### Process Improvements

**Improvement**: Start with "fit for purpose" requirements

- Set realistic quality and performance targets

#### 2. Practical Architecture Reviews
**Improvement**: Evaluate designs against "just good enough" criteria
